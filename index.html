About

Tunisia is where I was born in 1994. I am a Research Associate at SnT (SIGCOM), University of Luxembourg. Previously, I was a postdoctoral researcher at IMT Atlantique (STACK team), France (Jan 2023–Nov 2023). I received a Master’s degree (Web Intelligence) from ESEN, University of Manouba (2018), and a Ph.D. in Computer Science from LS2N, University of Nantes (Dec 2022). My interests include Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Federated Learning, Network Traffic Analysis, and the Internet of Things (IoT).
